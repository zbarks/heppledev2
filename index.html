Welcome to the Hepple Estate.

A very good drink in a very good place.

We distil spirits in the remote Northumbrian hills — quietly, carefully, and with a great deal of attention to what's growing outside.

The Estate

Four thousand acres of wild, quiet country.

Ancient juniper. Douglas fir. Water filtered through peat and limestone before it ever reaches our stills. Everything that goes into a bottle of Hepple begins here — and most of it doesn't travel very far.
