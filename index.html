The craft.

TURNING THE BEAUTY OF THE WILD LANDSCAPE INTO FLAVOUR.

01

SOURCING WILD BOTANICALS

OUR JUNIPER, DOUGLAS FIR AND BOG MYRTLE ARE HARVESTED FROM THE ESTATE ITSELF — PICKED AT THEIR PRIME AND CARRIED STRAIGHT TO THE STILL.

02

TRADITIONAL ONE-SHOT DISTILLATION

WE BEGIN WITH A ONE-SHOT LONDON DRY METHOD — A RICH, CREAMY FOUNDATION THAT MOST DISTILLERIES WOULD CONSIDER THE END OF THE JOB.

03

GLASS VACUUM DISTILLATION

OUR FRESHEST BOTANICALS GO INTO A GLASS VACUUM STILL AT 40°C — PRESERVING DELICATE, BRIGHT NOTES THAT HEAT WOULD OTHERWISE DESTROY.

04

SUPERCRITICAL EXTRACTION

JUNIPER BERRIES ARE HELD AT SUPERCRITICAL PRESSURE — A LIQUID AND GAS AT ONCE — TO DRAW OUT THEIR DEEPEST CEDAR AND SANDALWOOD CHARACTER.

05

BLEND, BOTTLE, SHIP

THREE EXPRESSIONS, BLENDED AND BOTTLED ON THE ESTATE. READY TO BE POURED — BY YOU, FOR WHOEVER YOU'RE WITH.
